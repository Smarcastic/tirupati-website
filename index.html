Who We Are

A Sacred Home Away From Home

International Reddy Bhavan is a premium pilgrim accommodation complex in Mangalam, Tirupati — thoughtfully designed for devotees who deserve comfort alongside their spiritual journey.

With more than 35 million pilgrims visiting Sri Venkateswara Temple every year, we understand that a peaceful, well-appointed stay is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Our spacious suites, restaurant, and banquet facilities ensure that every family, group, and solo pilgrim feels truly at home.

Situated in Mangalam — conveniently close to the railway station, bus stand, and key temples — International Reddy Bhavan offers the perfect base to explore Tirupati peacefully and without stress.

"

Devotees strongly believe that getting blessings from Lord Venkateshwara will lead to a wealthy and fulfilling future. We are honoured to be part of that sacred journey.

— D. Murali Krishna Reddy, Chairman

Where We Are

Mangalam, Tirupati

At the foot of the sacred Tirumala Hills, surrounded by the fresh air of Seshachalam Forest.

Our location in Mangalam places you within touching distance of every important landmark in Tirupati. Whether you are visiting for darshan, sightseeing, or simply seeking a peaceful retreat — International Reddy Bhavan is the ideal base.

Not only are we close to the railway station and bus stand, we are also immersed in the natural serenity of the Seshachalam Forest, ensuring that every breath you take is peaceful and pure.

Nearby Landmarks

  • Tirumala Sri Venkateswara Temple
  • Kapila Theertham
  • ISKON Temple
  • Tiruchanur — Sri Padmavathi Devi Temple (5 km)
  • Kanipakam — Lord Ganesha Temple (85 km)
  • Srikalahastti Temple (60 km)
  • Tirupati Railway Station
  • Tirupati Bus Stand
  • Tirupati Airport
